Music & Recording Program

Empowering at-risk youth through music creation, recording, and performance. Building confidence, developing skills, and transforming lives through the universal language of music.

Call: (619) 796-1844 Email Us
Music Programs

Sound of Transformation

Professional music education and recording opportunities that help youth find their voice and express their faith.

Beat Making & Production

Learn industry-standard recording software, beat-making, mixing, and mastering in our professional studio environment using the latest production tools.

Rap & Vocal Performance

Develop your rap flow, delivery, and vocal techniques while learning to express your story and faith through powerful hip-hop performance.

Lyricism & Songwriting

Master the art of writing compelling lyrics, develop your unique voice, and learn to craft songs that share your experiences and faith-based message.

Studio Recording & Engineering

Access professional recording equipment and learn audio engineering skills to create high-quality demos, mixtapes, and finished tracks.

Live Performance & Showcases

Build confidence and stage presence through live rap performances at community events, church services, and youth showcases.

A faith-based non-profit organization dedicated to transforming the lives of at-risk youth in San Diego County through mentorship, boxing, creative arts, and the love of Christ.
