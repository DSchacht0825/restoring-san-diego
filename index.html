H.I.T.S. Gang Prevention Curriculum

Evidence-based gang prevention program teaching Honor, Integrity, Tenacity, and Sacrifice. Specialized curriculum for at-risk youth developing character, life skills, and decision-making abilities to avoid gang involvement.

Boxing Programs for At-Risk Youth

Structured boxing and fitness programs providing discipline, mentorship and positive outlets for troubled youth. Combat sports training combined with gang intervention and character development in San Diego.

Creative Arts Youth Services

Art therapy and creative expression programs helping at-risk youth process trauma, build self-esteem and develop job skills. Music, painting, and digital arts provide positive alternatives to gang activity.
