Creative Arts Program

Empowering at-risk youth through artistic expression, music, and creative workshops. Building confidence, inspiring purpose, and transforming lives through the power of creativity.

Call: (619) 796-1844 Email Us
What We Offer

Drawing & Painting with Faith

Exploring color, texture, and technique while connecting creative work to personal stories of hope and redemption.

Painting with Purpose

Exploring color, texture, and technique while connecting creative work to personal stories of hope and redemption.

Drawing Out the Heart

Learning sketching skills while reflecting on Scripture and how God is shaping their lives.

Faith & Creativity Workshops

Guided sessions that pair biblical truths with hands-on projects, helping youth see the beauty of God's design in their own lives.

Community Art Projects

Collaborative murals and exhibits that share messages of faith, unity, and restoration with the wider community.

Why Faith + Art Matters

Art has the power to heal, inspire, and bring people together. For at-risk youth, it:

Opens the door for healing

by giving them a safe outlet to express their emotions.

Builds confidence

as they see their skills grow and their work appreciated.

Strengthens community

through shared projects that proclaim hope.

Reveals God's truth

that beauty can come from brokenness.

A faith-based non-profit organization dedicated to transforming the lives of at-risk youth in San Diego County through mentorship, boxing, creative arts, and the love of Christ.
