Our Curriculum

The H.I.T.S. Program

A comprehensive character development curriculum designed specifically for at-risk youth in San Diego.

The H.I.T.S. curriculum is the cornerstone of our youth development programs at Restoring San Diego. This faith-based character-building program focuses on four essential principles that help young people develop the strength, integrity, and perseverance needed to overcome life's challenges.

Developed through years of experience working with at-risk youth, H.I.T.S. combines biblical principles with practical life skills training. Each component of the program is designed to address the specific challenges and needs that our youth face on the streets of San Diego.

Through interactive lessons, real-life applications, and ongoing mentorship, participants learn to embody these four core values that can transform their lives and their communities.

Four Pillars

The H.I.T.S. Foundation

Each letter represents a crucial character trait that builds a foundation for lasting transformation.

HONOR

Respecting God, Others, and Yourself

Honor begins with understanding our worth as children of God. We teach youth to honor their Creator, respect authority figures, value their peers, and maintain self-respect. This foundation helps them make decisions that reflect their true identity in Christ.

  • Understanding God-given worth and dignity
  • Respecting parents, teachers, and mentors
  • Treating peers with kindness and respect
  • Making choices that honor their identity in Christ

INTEGRITY

Living Truth in Every Situation

Integrity means doing the right thing even when no one is watching. We help youth develop moral courage and ethical decision-making skills rooted in biblical truth. This character trait builds trust and opens doors to positive opportunities.

  • Developing moral courage and ethical thinking
  • Being honest in all relationships and situations
  • Keeping commitments and promises
  • Standing up for what is right, even when difficult

TENACITY

Never Giving Up on God's Plan

Tenacity is the determination to persevere through challenges and setbacks. We teach youth that obstacles are opportunities for growth and that God has a purpose for their lives worth fighting for. This resilience helps them overcome addiction, trauma, and hopelessness.

  • Developing resilience through faith in God's plan
  • Learning to overcome obstacles and setbacks
  • Building mental and emotional strength
  • Pursuing goals with determination and purpose

SACRIFICE

Putting Others Before Yourself

True leadership and maturity come through learning to serve others. We teach youth about Christ's ultimate sacrifice and how they can follow His example by putting the needs of others before their own. This transforms self-centered thinking into servant leadership.

  • Understanding Christ's sacrificial love
  • Serving family, friends, and community
  • Giving up harmful habits for positive change
  • Developing servant leadership qualities
