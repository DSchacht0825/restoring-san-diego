Meet Our Leaders

Our team brings firsthand experience of transformation through faith, providing authentic mentorship to at-risk youth.

Scott Miner

President & Founder

Scott's journey from the streets to leading a faith-based organization is a testament to the power of redemption and second chances. Having personally experienced the challenges that many of our youth face today, Scott brings authentic understanding and compassionate leadership.

Freedom Series Creator Street-to-Faith Testimony Youth Mentorship Expert

Adrian Vargas

Vice President

Adrian's transformation from gang member to professional boxer to faith-based leader exemplifies the incredible change possible through Christ's love. His journey gives him unique credibility and connection with the youth we serve.

Professional Boxer Gang-to-Grace Testimony Restoration Ringside Leader

Kayviana Mitchell

Chief Financial Officer

Kayviana is deeply passionate about inspiring youth and young adults, a calling shaped by her own transformative walk with Christ. Through seasons of challenge, she has learned to navigate with grace, rebuild through faith, and lead with authenticity rooted in spiritual grounding.

With a heart for discipleship and empowerment, Kayviana encourages young people to look beyond their current circumstances and embrace the calling God has placed on their lives. Her vision is to raise up a generation that knows Jesus personally, walks boldly in faith, and serves others with humility, compassion, and purpose.

Youth Empowerment Discipleship Leader Transformational Ministry

Daniel Guzman

Program Management Operations

Ernie Garabay

Outreach Director

Ernie's story is one of radical transformation. Raised on the streets and hardened by time in prison, he was once dismissed and labeled as anything but a leader. Yet leadership was always within him. Whether navigating the chaos of the streets or life behind bars, Ernie had a unique ability to draw people in, guide them, and earn their respect.

Today, Ernie leads with a renewed purpose: pointing people to Jesus Christ. A man of deep integrity, he lives a life rooted in faith, grace, and authenticity. His greatest strength lies in his ability to connect with youth—especially those who feel lost, angry, or unseen. His influence doesn't stem from titles or sermons, but from raw lived experience. He's been there. Done that. And he carries no judgment.

Through his honesty, humility, and unwavering faith, Ernie is living proof that redemption is real—and that no matter your past, you can become a powerful vessel for change.

Street-to-Faith Testimony Youth Connection Expert Redemption Story

Elodia Miner

Director of Creative Arts

Born and raised in San Diego, Elodia Miner has always carried a deep love for Jesus and a passion for serving others. With a professional background in dentistry, she spent years caring for people's physical needs with precision and compassion. But God began stirring something new in her heart—a calling to bring deeper, lasting healing, not just to the body, but to the soul.

Answering that call, Elodia transitioned into women's ministry, where she discovered the incredible power of creativity as a tool for restoration. She began using art—through painting, drawing, and creative expression—as a way to help women process their pain, tell their stories, and encounter the healing presence of God.

Her ministry focuses on creating safe spaces where women can experience God's truth, love, and acceptance while rediscovering their worth and identity in Christ.
