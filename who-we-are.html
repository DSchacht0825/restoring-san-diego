Our Mission

Transforming Streets Through Faith

We are dedicated to transforming the streets of San Diego by reaching at-risk youth with the love of Christ.

Restoring San Diego was born from a deep understanding that every young person deserves hope, mentorship, and the opportunity to break free from cycles of hardship. Our founders have walked the difficult paths that many of our youth face today, and through faith, they found transformation.

We believe that lasting change comes not just from addressing immediate needs, but from building character, instilling values, and providing the spiritual foundation necessary for true transformation. Our approach combines practical support with faith-based mentorship to create lasting impact in the lives of at-risk youth.

Through our various programs, we provide a safe haven where young people can discover their worth, develop their potential, and find the strength to overcome the challenges they face. We are committed to walking alongside each individual on their journey toward restoration and hope.
